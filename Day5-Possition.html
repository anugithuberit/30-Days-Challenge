Bugatti Veyron

Bugatti Veyron

The heart and soul of the VEYRON is the 8.0-liter W16 engine with four turbochargers, which originally generated 1,001 PS, and an incredible 1,200 PS in later models. The 16-cylinder mid-engine is 710 mm in length, no longer than a conventional V12 engine, and weighs just 490 kg due to its lightweight construction.
